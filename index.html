Improving The Quality Of Your Life Through Better Health.

Thirumala Hospitals, a polyclinic since 2003 with supreme focus on  primary and secondary care is established as a 30 bedded multi speciality hospital. An NABH certified hospital, Thirumala Hospital, is committed to create quality care beginning from admittance to evaluating patient satisfaction.

Find A Doctor

An epicentre with complete limelight on physical , mental and social well- being, our healthcare offerings are assisted by a team of concerned and committed medical expertise whose proficiency excels in the respective domains.

Our excellent surgical facilities and high quality surgical specialities, provided by our OT Department is acknowledged.The team comprises off trained nurses, OT technicians, assistants and sterile service staff.

The Diagnostic Unit is equipped to facilitate diagnosis and provides specialised tests for evaluation of patients.

  • Our HDU is specially staffed and equipped providing a level of care intermediate between intensive care and the general ward care, offering invasive monitoring and support for patients with or at risk of developing single-organ failure
  • Infrastructure is all about building assets for the patients, promoting improved standards of health care and wellbeing.
  • The environment is structured to be fit-for-purpose, with a high degree of sanitation and is adequately flexible to serve all patients.
